How to USE

Using the Philips Airfryer is simple and straightforward:

  1. Preheat the air fryer by setting the desired temperature and allowing it to run for a few minutes.
  2. Prepare your food by seasoning it as desired. You can use a small amount of oil if needed.
  3. Place the food in the air fryer basket, ensuring it is evenly spread out for optimal cooking.
  4. Select the appropriate cooking settings using the digital touchscreen. You can choose from various presets or manually set the time and temperature.
  5. Start the air fryer and let it cook your food. You may need to shake or turn the food halfway through the cooking process for even results.
  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, carefully remove the basket and transfer the food to a plate.
  7. Allow the air fryer to cool down before cleaning. The basket and other removable parts are dishwasher safe for easy cleaning.
